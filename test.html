ABOUT

Seawise Capital

Seawise Capital Limited is a UK based trade finance company, with a mission to make trade finance more accessible. We combine our expertise in Finance and Technology to bring Trade Finance into the digital age. We are an international company with a global presence, which means we can work with exporters and importers all over the world, and are able to service different types of businesses.


Our innovative approach to supply-chain finance means we can create funding solutions and structures that incumbents typically won’t offer. We pride ourselves on our service, and are always accessible to our customers. This means more flexibility for our clients along with an improved customer experience.

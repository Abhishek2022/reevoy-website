Why Invest?

By building an automated software platform that
underwrites and funds cross border trade for fast-growing
Asian exporters, Reevoy provides investors access
to short term trade finance receivables. We bring high
yielding results with low volatility to the table.

Non Recourse Export Factoring

Reevoy's working capital facility is tailored for exporters who
have exceeded their bank limits and do not want to invest in
collateral further, instead invest in growing the business. We
give you access to the required working capital which is
essential for you to grow your business, and our non-recourse
facilities help you mitigate the risks of international trade.
Our Offering helps you

  • Offer Credit Terms to your importers and not lose out orders.
  • Get paid for your invoices as soon as you ship instead of
    for it to get due and even protect your business from
    delayed payment.
  • Keeps you away from unpredictable, expensive and
    inefficient LC’s
  • Get a line of working capital credit over and above your
    bank’s credit facility.

- Easier and faster access to working capital.
